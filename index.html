Bodybuilding and Fitness Pro

I have 20 years of experience in the service industry as a Cosmetologist. After mastering a craft I love, I’ve discovered that it’s no longer a challenge to “do hair.” Although I excel at this art, I’ve realized that my true passion is to cultivate relationships with people and help them to be the best version of themselves. Over the past decade I have undergone a spiritual journey that has helped me to overcome drug addiction and totally transform my spiritual life. Through faith in a higher power I’ve been able to find self acceptance and self awareness. I have hope and faith that as long as I do the next right thing, I will continue to have a life beyond my wildest dreams. This is a gift I want to share with others.

Here begins “InspireBySKG.”

I’ve always had a love of the fitness industry and the art of transforming ones image. I’m excited to use the knowledge and experience I’ve gained over the years, my certification as a personal trainer and my hands-on interaction with empowering clients in this next chapter of growth.
